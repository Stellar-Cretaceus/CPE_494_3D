> "Khaenri'ah... didn't fall, did it? Since you're still here..."

## Concept
This program is inspired by Genshin Impact, depicting the Khaenri'ah kingdom the moment close to ruined. 
Cubes resulted from Unknown God (Shade of Space) floating indicates the incoming Destruction. We can feel cubes floating close and floating far away.
Blending the orbit of cubes with the cube in the background, making the 2D turns into 3D.

## Feature
- Background: 2D Plane map on a quad   
- Orbit Cube: Generated cubes that orbit around certain points, making the 2D picture becomes 3D
- Floating Cube: Generated cubes that goes up and down while spinning itself
